The Belgian Gazette

We constantly scan all tweets published by a panel of about 2000 multidisciplinary Belgian tweeps, mostly people who follow the news from close, people with a broad interest scope and people whose opnion matters. Every 15 minutes on the 15th minute, we filter out the 5 most important/popular entries and publish them here on The Belgian Gazette a.k.a. Ga-Z.

- We try to avoid tweets from people with extrimist opinions.
- We try to avoid marketing tweets.
- We will favor tweets containing terms that seem to matter for our public.
- We will devaluate tweets containing offensive words or terms that point to possible extremist or fundamentalist opinions

This way, we come up with a very limited set of tweets that will show what Belgian people esteem important at this moment.
